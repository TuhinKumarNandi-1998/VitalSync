## üéØ Overview

**VitalSync** is a comprehensive hospital management backend system designed for multi-department healthcare facilities.

### Key Capabilities

- Patient registration and medical history management  
- Appointment scheduling with doctor availability  
- OPD & IPD management  
- Pharmacy inventory & prescriptions  
- Laboratory tests & reports  
- Billing, invoicing & payments  
- Role-based access control (JWT)
